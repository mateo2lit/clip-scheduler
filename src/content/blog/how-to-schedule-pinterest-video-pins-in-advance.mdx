Pinterest is underused by most video creators — and that's exactly why it's worth being on. While every creator is fighting for attention on TikTok and Instagram, Pinterest video pins have long shelf lives, surface in search results for months, and drive consistent referral traffic. The problem is that Pinterest's native scheduling tools are clunky for video, and most cross-platform schedulers treat it as an afterthought.

This guide covers how to schedule Pinterest video pins in advance, how to set up boards correctly, and how to fold Pinterest into a broader multi-platform workflow.

## How Pinterest Video Pins Work

Pinterest is a visual search engine, not a social feed. That distinction matters for how you approach video content:

- **Pins surface in search results** — not just in followers' feeds. A well-optimized pin can get impressions for months or years.
- **Video pins autoplay silently** in the feed and search results. The visual hook has to work without audio.
- **Saves (repins) matter more than likes.** When someone saves your pin to a board, it extends the pin's reach to their followers.
- **Titles and descriptions are searchable.** Pinterest uses them to surface your pin in relevant searches — treat them more like SEO copy than a social caption.

## Pinterest Video Specs

Before scheduling, know the technical requirements:

| Spec | Limit |
|------|-------|
| File types | MP4, MOV, M4V |
| Max file size | 2 GB |
| Video length | 4 seconds to 15 minutes |
| Recommended aspect ratio | 9:16 (vertical) or 1:1 (square) |
| Min resolution | 240p |
| Recommended resolution | 1080p or higher |

Vertical video (9:16) performs best on mobile, where most Pinterest browsing happens. If you're repurposing a TikTok or Reel, the aspect ratio is already correct.

## Pinterest's Native Scheduler: What You're Working With

Pinterest has a built-in scheduler in Pinterest Business — but it has real limitations for video:

**What works:**
- Schedule pins up to 30 days in advance
- Basic title and description
- Board selection

**What's missing:**
- No bulk scheduling — one pin at a time
- No queue or recurring time slots
- No cross-platform posting
- Analytics on scheduled pins aren't visible until after they post
- Mobile scheduling is limited

For creators posting regularly, this becomes time-consuming fast. You're scheduling every pin individually, at a specific time, through Pinterest's interface only.

## Pinterest Video Strategy for Creators

**Think search-first.** Before writing a title, ask what someone would search for to find this content. A clip of a clutch Warzone play should be titled "Warzone Solo vs Squad Clutch" not "wait for it 😳".

**Post to relevant boards, not everything.** Saving a gaming clip to a "Home Decor" board because it happened to be your first board hurts more than it helps. Relevance matters.

**Repurpose your best performers.** Unlike TikTok or Instagram, Pinterest content has a long tail. A pin that starts getting saves can continue driving impressions for months. Post your best-performing content from other platforms to Pinterest.

**Consistent pinning beats burst pinning.** Pinterest rewards accounts that pin regularly. 1–3 pins per day performs better than 20 pins in one day followed by silence.

**Optimal posting times (general baseline):**
- 8–11 PM in your audience's time zone performs consistently well
- Saturday and Sunday tend to have higher engagement
- Avoid early morning weekdays

These vary by niche. Pinterest Analytics shows your audience's active times after a few weeks of data.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a Pinterest Business account?**
Yes. Pinterest's API access is only available for Business accounts. Converting a personal account to Business is free and takes about 30 seconds at pinterest.com/business/convert.

**Can I post to multiple Pinterest accounts?**
Yes. Connect as many accounts as you need in Settings and select which ones to post to on each upload.

**What happens if my Pinterest token expires?**
Clip Dash refreshes Pinterest tokens automatically before they expire. You won't need to reconnect unless you explicitly revoke access from your Pinterest account settings.

**Can I post to multiple boards at once?**
Not in one post — each pin goes to one board. If you want the same video on multiple boards, schedule it as separate posts with different board selections.

**Does Pinterest support Stories or Idea Pins through the API?**
Currently, the Pinterest API supports standard video pins. Idea Pins (the multi-frame format) are not available through the API for third-party scheduling.
